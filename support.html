FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about using the platform.

How do I create an event on Eventlyy?

After registering a host account, navigate to the Create Event page from your dashboard. Fill in your event details — name, date, venue, description, and ticket tiers — then publish. Your event will be live on the browse page within minutes.

What payment methods do you support?

We currently support Stripe, Paystack, and Flutterwave as payment gateways. During checkout, attendees can select their preferred provider. All transactions are processed securely through encrypted channels.

How do payouts work for event hosts?

Event revenue is collected and held securely. After your event concludes, payouts are processed to the bank account you configured in your host settings. The admin team reviews and approves disbursements on a scheduled cycle. A 5% ticketing fee may apply depending on your plan.

Can I get a refund for a ticket?

Refund policies are set by each event organizer. If you need a refund, contact the event host directly through your ticket confirmation email. For disputes, you can reach our support team at support@eventlyy.com.

How does ticket verification work at the gate?

Each ticket comes with a unique QR code. At the event venue, hosts or assigned staff use the Ticket Verification scanner on their dashboard to scan attendee codes. The system validates the ticket in real-time, preventing duplicates and unauthorized entry.

Is Eventlyy free to use?

Creating an account and browsing events is completely free. For event hosts, we offer a free tier for small community events. A small ticketing fee is applied to paid ticket sales to cover payment processing and platform maintenance costs.
